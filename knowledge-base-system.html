Operations Enablement Case Study

Turning scattered process knowledge into a scalable internal support system.

Built an internal knowledge base to organize SOPs, team-specific procedures, technology documentation, and operational resources so employees could find answers faster and follow consistent processes across the firm.

Organization Robbins Estate Law
Project Type Knowledge Management
Focus Areas SOPs · Training · Operational Scale
System HubSpot Knowledge Base

Challenge

As the firm grew, important process knowledge lived across people, documents, chats, and disconnected systems.

This made it harder for team members to find the correct procedure, follow consistent steps, and onboard into systems efficiently.

Solution

I built a centralized internal knowledge base organized by team and function, giving employees one place to find SOPs, system instructions, training materials, and operational guidance.

The system was designed to support both daily process execution and long-term organizational scalability.

Implementation

  • Structured the knowledge base by department and functional area
  • Created categories for Intake, Sales, Marketing, Operations, and Technology
  • Added SOP-style documentation for repeatable internal processes
  • Created guidance for using systems like HubSpot, Clio, Process Street, and Asana
  • Added links and buttons to make article creation easier for internal users
  • Designed the homepage layout to make key resources easier to access
  • Supported documentation standards for future article creation

Results

  • Created a central source of truth for internal procedures
  • Improved access to SOPs and system instructions
  • Reduced reliance on one-off questions for repeatable processes
  • Supported onboarding, training, and team consistency
  • Created a scalable documentation foundation for future growth
