My Story

From operations to automation.

My career has never followed a straight line — and that is exactly what makes my approach to systems, operations, and technology different.

Building structure has always been the throughline.

I grew up in San Antonio, Texas and graduated from Ronald Reagan High School in 2014. At 19, I joined the U.S. Navy as a Master at Arms. Although I originally tested high enough for IT, there were no spots available at the time, so I began my career in military law enforcement and security.

While stationed in Bangor, Washington supporting nuclear weapon security operations, I was later moved into a holding unit after becoming pregnant with my daughter. During that time, I worked directly with DoD employees conducting barracks inspections and developed a functional barracks inspection program. The program was adopted, praised by leadership, and later implemented at a neighboring base.

That experience earned me recognition including Blue Jacket of the Quarter and the Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medal. More importantly, it showed me something that has followed me throughout my career: I naturally see broken processes, organize them, and turn them into systems people can actually use.

Every role taught me how operations really work.

Joined Robbins Estate Law and helped scale its technology ecosystem across HubSpot, Clio, Process Street, Zapier, Jotform, Asana, and reporting tools.

Where I am today.

Today, I lead technology systems work at Robbins Estate Law, where I help support a growing multi-location law firm through CRM architecture, automation, integrations, documentation, reporting, and internal support systems.

Since joining the firm, I have helped support major operational growth — from a smaller two-location firm with limited automation infrastructure to a larger organization with thousands of CRM records, hundreds of automations, and a dedicated technology support structure.

My work now includes managing and building across HubSpot, Zapier, Clio, Process Street, Jotform, Asana, Power BI, and custom API integrations.
