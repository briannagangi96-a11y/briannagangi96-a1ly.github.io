Featured Case Study

HubSpot CRM Implementation at Robbins Estate Law

Led the full implementation and rollout of HubSpot as the firm’s central CRM, intake, sales, automation, and reporting system — completed in approximately three months while supporting a growing multi-location law firm.

Challenge

Robbins Estate Law needed a stronger, more scalable CRM foundation to support intake, sales, reporting, follow-up, marketing visibility, and operational accountability across multiple firm locations.

Solution

I helped architect, configure, and launch HubSpot as the firm’s core business system, including pipelines, properties, workflows, automations, reporting, team processes, and cross-system integrations.

My Role

I served as the internal systems lead responsible for implementation planning, CRM structure, automation design, data organization, user adoption, process documentation, and ongoing optimization.

Impact

The implementation created a centralized source of truth for intake, sales, matter visibility, automation, reporting, and client journey management.

3 Months Implementation Timeline
300+ HubSpot Workflows Managed
25,000+ Deals & Leads Supported
6–7 Firm Locations Supported

Key Work Completed

  • Built and organized HubSpot pipelines for intake, sales, and operational visibility.
  • Created custom properties to support reporting, automation, and business processes.
  • Designed and managed hundreds of workflows for routing, reminders, updates, and internal processes.
  • Supported user adoption across intake, sales, marketing, operations, attorneys, and leadership.
  • Improved CRM structure so leadership could better track performance, follow-up, and firm growth.
  • Created the foundation for future integrations with Clio, Jotform, Power BI, Asana, and other tools.
