Featured Case Study

HubSpot CRM Implementation at Robbins Estate Law

Led the implementation and rollout of HubSpot as the firm's central CRM, intake, sales, automation, and reporting platform. The project established the operational foundation needed to support a rapidly growing multi-location law firm while improving visibility, accountability, and scalability across departments.

Client / Organization Robbins Estate Law
Project Type CRM Implementation
Focus Areas Automation · Reporting · Legal Operations
Platform HubSpot CRM

Challenge

As Robbins Estate Law continued to grow, the firm needed a centralized system capable of supporting:

  • Intake operations
  • Sales processes
  • Client lifecycle management
  • Reporting and analytics
  • Cross-department visibility
  • Automation at scale

The existing systems lacked consistency, making it difficult to maintain operational visibility, standardize processes, and support future growth initiatives.

Strategy

Rather than treating HubSpot as a simple CRM implementation, the project focused on designing a scalable operational ecosystem.

The implementation strategy included:

  • CRM architecture design
  • Pipeline development
  • Data structure planning
  • Automation strategy
  • Reporting framework creation
  • Team adoption planning
  • Future integration readiness

The goal was to create a system that could support the firm's current operations while providing a foundation for future automation and reporting initiatives.

Implementation

Key work completed during the implementation included:

  • Built and configured HubSpot CRM from the ground up
  • Designed custom pipelines supporting intake and sales operations
  • Created custom properties for operational reporting
  • Developed automation infrastructure using HubSpot workflows
  • Established standardized data collection processes
  • Built reporting dashboards for leadership visibility
  • Implemented process documentation and internal training
  • Supported organization-wide user adoption

The implementation was completed in approximately three months.

Results

The project established a scalable CRM foundation that continues to support daily operations across the organization.

Key Outcomes

  • HubSpot fully implemented in approximately 3 months
  • 300+ workflows created and maintained
  • 25,000+ CRM records supported
  • 600+ active legal matters supported
  • 6–7 firm locations supported
  • Foundation created for future Clio, Jotform, Power BI, and API integrations

Technology Stack

  • HubSpot CRM
  • Zapier
  • Clio Manage
  • Jotform
  • Power BI
  • Asana

My Role

As the internal technology and systems leader, I was responsible for:

  • CRM architecture
  • Process design
  • Automation strategy
  • Workflow development
  • Reporting structure
  • Documentation
  • User adoption
  • Ongoing optimization

The project remains one of the foundational technology initiatives supporting the firm's continued growth.

  • Built and organized HubSpot pipelines for intake, sales, and operational visibility.
  • Created custom properties to support reporting, automation, and business processes.
  • Designed and managed hundreds of workflows for routing, reminders, updates, and internal processes.
