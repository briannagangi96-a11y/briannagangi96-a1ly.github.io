Jotform Intake Automation Platform

A custom intake automation system built to streamline form generation, client prefill, submission tracking, PDF storage, and HubSpot deal updates.

Automation Case Study

Creating a smoother intake experience for clients and internal teams.

Built a custom Jotform and HubSpot intake automation platform that helps the firm generate client-specific intake forms, prefill known information, track submission status, and store completed form data directly on HubSpot deals.

Organization Robbins Estate Law
Project Type Intake Automation
Focus Areas Client Experience · Forms · CRM Automation
Systems Jotform · HubSpot

Challenge

The firm needed a more scalable way to send intake forms, collect client information, track completion, and keep completed documents connected to the correct HubSpot deal.

Manual form handling created extra administrative work and made it harder to maintain consistent visibility into where each client stood in the intake process.

Solution

I built a custom intake automation platform connecting HubSpot and Jotform so internal users could generate the correct intake form from a HubSpot deal and track the full submission lifecycle.

The system supports form links, client prefill, submission updates, completed PDFs, uploaded files, and workflow-triggered automation.

Implementation

  • Built a custom HubSpot CRM card for intake form generation
  • Connected HubSpot deals to Jotform forms using form-specific links
  • Created form prefill logic for client and related party information
  • Created HubSpot deal properties for form links, statuses, PDFs, and files
  • Configured Jotform webhook handling for submitted forms
  • Uploaded completed PDFs and attachments back into HubSpot
  • Created status tracking for Not Sent, Sent, and Submitted forms
  • Supported multiple form types across different legal service workflows

Results

  • Reduced manual intake form handling
  • Improved visibility into client form completion
  • Centralized completed form PDFs and attachments in HubSpot
  • Created a repeatable process for sending and tracking intake forms
  • Supported a more consistent client intake experience
  • Established a scalable foundation for future intake automation
